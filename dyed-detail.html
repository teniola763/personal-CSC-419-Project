Project Overview

Full Description

The Dyed Hair category focuses on bold color moments ‚Äî from soft honey tones to vibrant reds and pinks. These styles are part of the Virtual Hairstyle Gallery app so users can preview color looks before committing.

Color is self-expression. This collection highlights different shades and textures while keeping the UI clean and consistent for a premium feel.

Problem Solved

The Challenge: Dyeing your hair is a big commitment, especially with bold colors. Many people are unsure if a color will suit their skin tone or personal style.

The Solution: Our gallery showcases different hair colors, giving them confidence before making a permanent change.
